## Legacy Rescue & Technical Auditing
In the GovTech ecosystem, not every project is a 'greenfield' build. I was brought in to stabilize an existing application ('Lumen Gestão') heavily burdened with technical debt. The system suffered from critical race conditions, a monolithic codebase, and an infrastructure ill-suited for strict government data compliance.

## Surgical Engineering
- **Concurrency Battles:** Diagnosed and resolved massive Race Conditions in the Document Chat module. I implemented `RxJS Observables` and Reactive Patterns to guarantee frontend data consistency over unstable state lines.
- **Refactoring:** Handled refactoring on monolithic TypeScript blocks and fixed layout responsiveness affecting legacy displays.

## Strategic Roadmapping (The Vision)
Conducted a 150+ hour deep-dive technical audit demonstrating mathematically (via ROI & TCO) why the stack was financially unsustainable for the government scale. Designed the entire transition plan from Firebase to Single-Tenant Laravel & Docker clusters with strictly enforced 2FA and Audit Logs required by Law.
