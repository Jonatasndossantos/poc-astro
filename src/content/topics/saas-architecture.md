SaaS Architecture requires a deep understanding of multi-tenancy models, secure data partitioning, and performance optimization for heavy-load requests.

### Core Focus
- **Multi-Tenancy**: Single-tenant isolated environments vs. complex shared-schema SaaS.
- **Microservices & Modules**: Decoupling monoliths into scalable, reactive components.
- **Business Logic Complexity**: Managing multi-level hierarchies (e.g., City Halls $\to$ Sectors) with legal prerequisite validation.
